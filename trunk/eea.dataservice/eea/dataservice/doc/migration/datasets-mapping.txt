Info:
Relation property lookup on [ROD, CSI, datasets (plone), reports (plone), external URL]

Relations should be handled in a smart way :) to be discussed.

When the products that are related are different, the relation type can be automatiocally discovered by looking at the hierarchi of information products:

 ^ 1. Article, Highlight, PressRelease, Speech  (in plone)
 | 2. Publication (in plone)
 | 3. Indicator (in plone)
 | 4. Maps, Graphs (in plone later)
 | 5. Dataset (in plone later)
 | 6. Data Table (in plone later)
 | 7. Data File (in plone later)
 | 8. ROD (not in plone)

if a product A is above a product B, then looking on A page "Based on products: B"
when looking at B the page will have a relation to A saying "Derived products: A"

If the relation is from same type of information the relation needs to be specified by the Content Manager,
for example a CM would choose dataset D1 to be based on another dataset D2 the relation is
  D1 -> D2 = based on
  D1 <- D2 = derived
