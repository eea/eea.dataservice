Convert all EEAFigureFiles
==============================================================

There are two ways you can call this view, one only to see
the status of all EEAFigureFiles:

    http://www.eea.europa.eu/@@checkFiguresConvertion?info=1

And a second type of call (recomanded for cron jobs) to convert
all not already converted EEAFigureFiles:

    http://www.eea.europa.eu/@@checkFiguresConvertion
